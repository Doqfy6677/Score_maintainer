Welcome to Cricket Score Tracker!

This application helps you track cricket match scores with detailed team and player statistics. Here's how to use it:

Getting Started

  1. Create a Team: On the home screen, enter a team name and click "Create Team".
  2. Add Players: Click on a team to open the team screen, then add players by entering their names.
  3. Start Scoring: Click on a player to open the scoring interface.
  4. Record Scores: Use the scoring buttons to add runs, wickets, and extras.
  5. View Summary: Click "Team Summary" to see detailed statistics for the team and all players.

Key Features

Team Management

Create multiple teams and manage their players.

Player Management

Add and remove players from teams.

Detailed Scoring

Track runs, sixes, fours, and wickets for each player.

Extras Tracking

Record wide balls and no balls for accurate team scores.

Best Player Highlights

Automatically identifies best batsman and bowler.

Comprehensive Summary

View detailed statistics for the entire team.

Scoring Guide

Batting Scores

  • Six: Adds 6 runs to the player's score.
  • Four: Adds 4 runs to the player's score.
  • Run: Adds custom number of runs (1-6) to the player's score.

Bowling Scores

  • Wicket: Adds a wicket to the player's bowling statistics.

Extras

  • Wide Ball: Adds 1 run to the team's total score (not to any player).
  • No Ball: Adds 1 run to the team's total score (not to any player).

Themes

The app comes with three beautiful themes that you can switch between:

  • Neon: The default theme with bright neon colors.
  • Sunset: A warm theme with orange and red gradients.
  • Ocean: A cool theme with blue and teal colors.

Click on the theme buttons in the top-right corner to change the theme.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally in your browser using Local Storage. This means:

  • Your data persists between sessions
  • No internet connection is required after the initial page load
  • Your data stays private on your device

Note: Clearing your browser data will erase all stored teams and scores.
